Common Op-amp Topology that is used to add voltage [[gain(TODO)]] to a signal. The Topology has the signal go through the negative input of the op-amp and also contains negative feedback to make it so the gain is influenced/controlled by external components; Allowing for control on the amount of voltage gain induced onto a signal.
